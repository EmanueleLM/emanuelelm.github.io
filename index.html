Postdoctoral Researcher · University of Oxford · Alan Turing Institute

Emanuele La Malfa

I'm a postdoctoral researcher working with Prof. Michael Wooldridge and Prof. Nigel Shadbolt, exploring how to benchmark large language models in complex settings. My Ph.D., completed in 2023 under Prof. Marta Kwiatkowska, examined robustness at the intersection of NLP, computational linguistics, and linguistics.

I argue that the standard machine-learning view of robustness is ill-posed for language. I'm always keen to chat about collaborations, ideas, or curious questions—drop me a line or browse my publications for recent work.
