Postdoctoral Researcher · University of Oxford · Alan Turing Institute

Emanuele La Malfa

I'm a research associate at Oxford, working with Michael Wooldridge, exploring distributed reasoning and benchmarking, with a focus on language models. I earned my Ph.D. at Oxford in 2023 under Marta Kwiatkowska, on local robustness as a property of language for NLP.

I'm also the co-founder and co-director of the Institute for Decentralized AI (IDAI), a Cosmos research institute focusing on decentralised AI systems and distributed intelligence.

I'm always keen to chat about collaborations, ideas, or curious questions—drop me a line or browse my publications for recent work.
