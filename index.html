About

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Oxford and the Alan Turing Institute. I work with Prof. Michael Wooldridge and Prof. Nigel Shadbolt on benchmarking large language models. My Ph.D., completed in 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Marta Kwiatkowska, explored robustness at the intersection of NLP, computational linguistics, and linguistics.

I hold that robustness as commonly formulated in machine learning is ill-posed for language. Curious about details, collaborations, or just a chat? Reach out by email or browse my publications.
